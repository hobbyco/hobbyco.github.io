Essential Winning Tips

1. Remember 'Jacks or Better'

Payouts start from a pair of Jacks (J, Q, K, A). Low pairs don't win, so it's often better to discard them to chase a higher-ranking hand.

2. Chasing the Royal Flush

If you are only one card away from a Royal Flush, it is mathematically superior to break an existing winning hand (like a Flush or Straight) to go for it.

3. When You Have Nothing

If no winning pattern is visible, hold only the 'High Cards' (J or higher) and replace everything else.
